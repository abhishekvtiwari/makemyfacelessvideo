Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026  ·  Effective: April 15, 2026

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We do not sell your personal data — ever.

1. Who We Are

MakeMyFacelessVideo ("we", "us", "our") operates the website makemyfacelessvideo.com and the associated AI video creation platform. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@makemyfacelessvideo.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Email address — when you join our waitlist or create an account
  • Account information — name, profile details when you sign up via Google OAuth
  • Payment information — billing details processed securely by Stripe (international) or Razorpay (India). We never store your card details directly.
  • Video prompts and content — topics and scripts you submit to generate videos

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, time spent, clicks
  • Device data — browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • IP address — used for approximate location and security purposes
  • Cookies — session cookies for authentication and preference cookies for settings

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the MakeMyFacelessVideo platform
  • To send waitlist and launch notifications you opted into
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send product updates, tips, and feature announcements (you can opt out anytime)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your video prompts or generated content to train AI models without your explicit consent.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe / Razorpay — to process payments securely
  • Google — for OAuth authentication if you sign in with Google
  • ElevenLabs — your text content is sent to generate voiceovers
  • Pexels / Pollinations — keywords are sent to fetch stock footage and AI images
  • Cloudflare — for CDN, storage (R2), and DDoS protection
  • Analytics providers — anonymised usage data only

All third-party providers are bound by their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email
  • Object — object to processing of your data for direct marketing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@makemyfacelessvideo.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes.

7. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for login sessions and security
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and language preferences
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site (anonymised)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly without them.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and encrypted storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

9. Children's Privacy

MakeMyFacelessVideo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
