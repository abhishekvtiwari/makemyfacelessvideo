Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026 · Effective date: May 1, 2026

Summary: We collect only what we need to operate the service. We never sell your data. You can request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@makemyfacelessvideo.com.

1. Who We Are

MakeMyFacelessVideo ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered faceless video creation platform operated by MakeMyFacelessVideo.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information about you when you use our website and services.

If you have questions, contact us at privacy@makemyfacelessvideo.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you sign up (via Google OAuth or email).
  • Billing information — payment details processed securely via Razorpay (India) or Stripe (international). We never store your full card number.
  • Content you create — video topics, scripts, and settings you enter when generating videos.
  • Communications — emails or messages you send us for support.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, videos generated, and time spent on the platform.
  • Device data — browser type, operating system, IP address, and referring URL.
  • Cookies — session cookies for authentication and analytics cookies (see Section 7).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the MakeMyFacelessVideo service.
  • Process payments and manage your subscription.
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, password resets, feature updates you requested).
  • Send marketing emails only if you explicitly opted in — you can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your video content to train AI models, share it with third parties for advertising, or sell it in any form.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted third parties who help us operate the platform (Anthropic for AI generation, ElevenLabs for voice synthesis, Pexels for stock footage, Cloudflare for storage and CDN, Razorpay and Stripe for payments). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Generated videos are stored on Cloudflare R2 and retained for 90 days after creation unless you choose to delete them earlier or extend storage through your account settings.

When you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to fix inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email we send.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@makemyfacelessvideo.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies — required for authentication and security. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how the platform is used (e.g. page views, feature usage). You can opt out via your browser settings or by emailing us.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

9. Children's Privacy

MakeMyFacelessVideo is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Transfers

MakeMyFacelessVideo operates globally. Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States and India. We rely on standard contractual clauses and other appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice on the website at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Your continued use of the service after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
